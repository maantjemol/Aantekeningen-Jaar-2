# Big P's (HEAD)

- Heroism: This purpose involves challenging the status quo and achieving ambitious goals. Examples include Ford.
- Excellence: This purpose is about achieving the highest standards and quality in everything the company does
- Altruism: This purpose focuses on serving others and the greater good. Examples provided include Unilever
- Discovery: This purpose is centred around innovation, learning, and pushing boundaries. Examples include Google and Harvard. Google's purpose is stated as "to invent how to organize the world’s information and make it universally accessible and useful"

# Small P's (MLSW)

Magician: The Magician is the visionary innovator who transforms possibilities into reality through creativity and insight.
Lover: The Lover is the empathetic and passionate leader who cultivates connection and loyalty through emotional intelligence.
Sovereign: The Sovereign is the dignified leader who inspires unity and purpose by embodying self-awareness and serving the collective good.
Warrior: The Warrior is the disciplined and courageous leader who drives results through strength and focused action.
